About FreeBoard


FreeBoard is a place to share links about almost everything. The goal of this platform is to give users a lot of different content. The more people use FreeBoard, the more it becomes interesting.
There is no likes. No comments. This way, you are not only staring at "trends" or "hot". Every post have the same value as another one, despite of the content.
We want to remind you that everything here is anonymous. But if you don't respect the rules, you will be banned.

Have a good time on FreeBoard !
-The FreeBoard team
